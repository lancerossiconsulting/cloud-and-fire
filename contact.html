Where We Are

Flint Hills Cloud & Fire Fellowship is based in Manhattan, Kansas — the Flint Hills region of northeast Kansas. Cohort sessions are held in-person, with remote participation options under development for future cohorts.

Manhattan is home to Kansas State University, Fort Riley, and a wide range of congregations across the full spectrum of American Christian tradition. It is a community that already knows how to hold difference — and how much work that takes.
